My Favourite Place is Swat

Swat is a beautiful valley located in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. It is known for its stunning landscapes, lush green valleys, and serene rivers. The place is often referred to as the "Switzerland of the East" due to its picturesque scenery.
